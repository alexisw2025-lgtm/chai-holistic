🛍 Shopify CSV Export

When you're ready to set up Shopify, export your full product catalog as a Shopify-ready CSV. Upload it directly to Shopify Admin → Products → Import and all 81 products will be created with correct prices, SKUs, and descriptions in one click.

📋 How to set up Shopify

1
Go to shopify.com and create a new store. Choose the Basic plan ($39/mo) — it includes everything you need.
2
In your Shopify Admin, go to Products → Import and upload the CSV you downloaded above. All 81 products will be created with prices and SKUs.
3
Set up Shopify Payments (free, powered by Stripe). Go to Settings → Payments. This gives you 0% transaction fees on your plan.
4
Install the Buy Button channel from the Shopify App Store (free). This generates embed codes you can add to your existing chaiholistic.com site.
5
Paste the Buy Button embed codes into your React components where the "Add to Basket" buttons are. Your beautiful custom site stays — Shopify handles the checkout.
6
Add your Shopify product IDs back here under Settings → enter them in the shopify_id column. Future CSV exports will keep products synced.

💳 Shopify Payments setup

Once Shopify Payments is active, you'll accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay with zero additional transaction fees on the Basic plan. Processing fee is 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction — standard for all major processors.

✓
Go to Settings → Payments → Shopify Payments → Complete account setup. You'll need your business bank account and EIN (or SSN for sole proprietor).
